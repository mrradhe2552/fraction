fraction will enable a completely decentralised web where users are in control.

fraction is built to connect private and consortium chains, public and permissionless networks, oracles, and future technologies that are yet to be created. fraction facilitates an internet where independent blockchains can exchange information and transactions in a trustless way via the fraction relay chain.

fraction makes it easier than ever to create and connect decentralized applications, services, and institutions. By empowering innovators to build better solutions, we seek to free society from its reliance on a broken web where its large institutions can’t violate our trust.
